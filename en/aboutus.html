About us

Welcome to my website! My name is Alex Ringenbach, a first-year student at the BUT MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions). This website was created as part of a university project on a fascinating and current topic: malicious robots. Through this website, I invite you to explore different facets of the representation of robots in popular culture, as well as their potential impact on our world. My main articles focus on:

The novel "Into the Pit", which delves into a dark universe where machines are far from harmless.

The movie "Transformers 3", where giant robots play a central role in a battle between good and evil.

The book "Robots, Men and Peace", borrowed from the University Library, which addresses the ethical and philosophical challenges posed by robots in our society.

I also created a timeline tracing the evolution of robots, from the first automatons to the latest technological advances of our time. This project allows me to share my passion for new technologies while exploring the challenges they can pose to humanity.
