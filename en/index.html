Our Website

Welcome to the world of robots: between history, culture and imagination! Dive into a world where machines come to life, whether they are the result of human ingenuity or the collective imagination. Explore fascinating articles that highlight the role of robots in our stories and our daily lives. Discover:

  • "Five Nights at Freddy's", a captivating book that takes you into a world where robots are both fascinating and terrifying.
  • "Transformers 3", a cult film where alien machines wage an epic battle to save (or destroy) the Earth.
  • "Humans, Robots and Peace", an in-depth reflection on the future of coexistence between humans and robots.
  • Romeo, the moving character from the video game "Lies of P", which reinvents the story of Pinocchio from a mechanical angle.
